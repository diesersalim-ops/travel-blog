FAQ

What's the story behind my travel blog? ▼

It started as a way to keep memories from turning into blurry photo albums. What began as personal notes from backpacking trips slowly grew into a full travel journal – now shared with everyone chasing the same wanderlust.

How do I plan, document, and share my travels? ▼

Every trip starts with a rough route sketched on paper, refined with local recommendations once I arrive. I document everything in real time – photos, voice notes, quick sketches – then turn it into structured stories once I'm home.

How can brands, companies, or fellow creators collaborate with me or get in touch? ▼

I'm always open to collaborations that align with slow, sustainable travel. Reach out through the contact form below with your idea, and I'll get back to you within a few days.

How can readers support this blog and the work behind it? ▼

Share the articles you enjoy, leave a comment, or simply come back for the next story. If you'd like to support directly, every bit of engagement helps keep this blog independent.
